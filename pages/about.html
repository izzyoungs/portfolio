About me


Izzy is a Masters student in Urban and Regional Planning at Georgetown University. Her interests include data-driven political advocacy and urban analysis. She aspires to responsibly utilize data to contribute to policy solutions, particularly around affordable housing and housing finance.

Izzy also holds a Bachelors degree in Political Science and Communication Studies from the Unviersity of Nevada, Reno, and enjoys hiking, hot springs, rock climbing, science fiction, and public transit.
